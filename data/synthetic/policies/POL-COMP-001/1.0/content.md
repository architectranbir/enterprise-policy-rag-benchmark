# Gifts and Conflicts of Interest Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-COMP-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 1 May 2026  
**Owner:** Compliance  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy prevents gifts, hospitality and personal interests from improperly influencing decisions in the fictional organisation.

## 2. Gifts and hospitality limit

Employees may give or receive modest business gifts or hospitality worth no more than GBP 75 from one source in a twelve-month period. Anything above the limit requires prior Compliance approval.

## 3. Prohibited benefits

Cash, cash equivalents, personal discounts unavailable to colleagues, lavish entertainment and any benefit intended to influence a decision are prohibited regardless of value.

## 4. Public officials

Any gift or hospitality involving a public official requires prior written Compliance approval, even when its value is below GBP 75.

## 5. Gifts register

Permitted gifts or hospitality worth GBP 25 or more must be entered in the gifts register within five working days of being offered, given, accepted or declined.

## 6. Conflicts of interest

Employees must disclose actual, potential or perceived conflicts as soon as they arise and review their declaration annually. A manager and Compliance must document any management plan.

## 7. Breaches and advice

Suspected breaches must be reported through Compliance or the confidential speak-up channel. Employees should request advice before acting when they are uncertain.
