# Travel and Expenses Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-FIN-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 1 January 2025  
**Owner:** Finance  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy sets the fictional organisation's 2025 rules for reasonable and necessary business travel expenditure.

## 2. Travel class

Economy class is required for all flights under eight hours. Premium economy may be approved for longer flights; business class is not reimbursable under this version.

## 3. Booking requirements

Travel must be booked through the approved travel portal at least seven calendar days before departure whenever practical. The traveller's manager must approve the itinerary before purchase.

## 4. Accommodation

The maximum reimbursable hotel room rate is GBP 150 per night including taxes. A higher rate requires written Finance approval before booking.

## 5. Meals

Employees may claim actual meal costs up to GBP 45 per day during approved overnight travel. Alcohol and minibar purchases are not reimbursable.

## 6. Receipts and submission

Itemised receipts are required for individual expenses of GBP 20 or more. Claims must be submitted through the expense system within 30 calendar days after travel ends.

## 7. Exceptions

Exceptions must be documented and approved by both the budget owner and Finance before the cost is committed.
