# Supplier Purchasing Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-PROC-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 15 January 2026  
**Owner:** Procurement  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines fair, controlled and auditable purchasing from external suppliers in the fictional organisation.

## 2. Approval thresholds

Purchases up to GBP 5,000 require budget-owner approval. Purchases above GBP 5,000 require Procurement approval, and purchases above GBP 50,000 also require Finance Director approval.

## 3. Competitive quotations

At least three written quotations are required for purchases above GBP 10,000 unless Procurement approves a documented single-source justification before supplier selection.

## 4. Supplier due diligence

Before onboarding, suppliers must complete sanctions, financial, security, privacy and conflict checks proportionate to the service. High-risk findings require approval from the relevant control owner.

## 5. Conflicts of interest

Employees must disclose any personal, family or financial relationship with a prospective supplier and must not participate in selection or approval until the conflict is resolved.

## 6. Purchase orders

A valid purchase order must be issued before goods or services are ordered. Splitting a purchase to avoid an approval threshold or quotation requirement is prohibited.

## 7. Emergency purchasing

Emergency purchasing is allowed only to protect safety, service continuity or critical assets. The requester must notify Procurement within one working day and complete retrospective documentation within five working days.
