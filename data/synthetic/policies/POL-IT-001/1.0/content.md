# Acceptable Technology Use Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-IT-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 1 March 2026  
**Owner:** Information Technology  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines responsible use of devices, networks, software and technology services supplied by the fictional organisation.

## 2. Limited personal use

Occasional personal use is permitted when it is lawful, does not interfere with work, creates no material cost and does not expose the organisation to security or reputational risk.

## 3. Software installation

Users must not install software or browser extensions unless they are available through the approved software catalogue or Information Technology has granted written approval.

## 4. Generative artificial intelligence

Public generative-AI services must not receive confidential, restricted, personal or client information. Organisation-approved AI services may be used only for authorised purposes with required human review.

## 5. Privileged access

Administrator privileges must use a separate named account and may be exercised only for approved support or engineering tasks. Privileged credentials must not be used for email or routine browsing.

## 6. Monitoring and privacy

The organisation may log and monitor use of its technology to protect security, investigate misuse and meet legal obligations. Monitoring must be proportionate and handled under the privacy notice.

## 7. Return of assets

Devices, access cards and other technology assets must be returned by the employee's final working day or within two working days after a written request, whichever is earlier.
