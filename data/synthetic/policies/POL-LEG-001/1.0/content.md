# Records Retention and Disposal Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-LEG-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 1 April 2026  
**Owner:** Legal  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy ensures that fictional organisational records are retained only as long as needed and disposed of securely.

## 2. Retention schedule

Record owners must classify records against the approved retention schedule. Local copies must not be kept longer than the authoritative record unless Legal approves an exception.

## 3. Employment records

Core employment and payroll records must be retained for seven years after employment ends. Unsuccessful recruitment records must be deleted after twelve months unless consent supports a longer period.

## 4. Financial records

Invoices, expense claims, tax records and supporting accounting evidence must be retained for eight complete financial years after the relevant reporting period closes.

## 5. Legal hold

A legal hold immediately suspends normal deletion for all identified records, including duplicates. Only Legal may release a hold in writing.

## 6. Secure disposal

Expired electronic records must be irreversibly deleted through an approved process. Paper containing internal or more sensitive information must be placed in locked confidential-waste containers.

## 7. Backups

Backups are for recovery and do not extend the business retention period. Expired records may remain in encrypted backups until the normal backup cycle overwrites them, provided they are not restored for ordinary use.
