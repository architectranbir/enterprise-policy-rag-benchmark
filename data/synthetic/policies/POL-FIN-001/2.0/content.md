# Travel and Expenses Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-FIN-001  
**Version:** 2.0  
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026  
**Owner:** Finance  
**Classification:** Internal  

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the fictional organisation's current controls for safe, economical and properly authorised business travel.

## 2. Travel class

Economy class is required for flights under six hours. Premium economy may be approved for flights of six hours or more. Business class requires executive and Finance approval and is limited to flights exceeding ten hours.

## 3. Booking requirements

Travel must be booked through the approved travel portal at least fourteen calendar days before departure whenever practical. Manager approval is required before any ticket is purchased.

## 4. Accommodation

The maximum reimbursable hotel room rate is GBP 180 per night including taxes. Written Finance approval is required before booking above the limit.

## 5. Meals

Employees may claim actual meal costs up to GBP 55 per day during approved overnight travel. Alcohol, minibar items and entertainment are not reimbursable.

## 6. Receipts and submission

Itemised receipts are required for individual expenses of GBP 15 or more. Claims must be submitted through the expense system within 21 calendar days after travel ends.

## 7. Exceptions

An exception must be approved in writing by the budget owner and Finance before commitment. Emergency exceptions must be documented within two working days after the traveller becomes able to report them.
